ETERNAL SUNSHINE


Joel visits Rakunasa, who only erases painful memories, and decides to erase the memories of his ex-lover, Clementine.

As memories fade away, Joel just doesn't want to erase the happy memories, the memories that are imprinted in his heart... Will this pain go away if I erase you? Love is remembered like that again.
